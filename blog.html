Naruto

Naruto is an immensely popular anime series that originated from a manga created by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with a dream of becoming the strongest ninja in his village and attaining the title of Hokage, the leader of the village. It is one of the finest anime series I've ever watched.
